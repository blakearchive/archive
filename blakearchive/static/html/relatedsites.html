Selected Blake Sites

Blake Digital Text Project: an online edition and concordance of David V. Erdman's The Complete Poetry and Prose of William Blake; by Nelson Hilton; from Arizona State University

The Blake Society: a society for the appreciation of the work of William Blake

The British Library's Virtual Books Page: allows users to page through virtual representations of texts, including the notebook of William Blake

Discovering Literature: Romantics and Victorians: featuring manuscript materials, teaching resources, articles, and instructional videos related to a number of authors, including William Blake; from the British Library

Editions of Blake's Illuminated Books from the William Blake Trust: ordering information for the facsimile series published by Princeton University Press

G. E. Bentley: Blake Collection: contains the collection catalog of the G. E. Bentley Collection of William Blake and His Contemporaries at the Victoria University Library, as well as Bentley's William Blake and His Circle: Publications and Discoveries from 1992 and Sale Catalogues of William Blake's Works in .pdf format

An Island in the Moon: video, introduction, photographs, and text of the 1983 Cornell University theatrical production by Joseph Viscomi, with music by Margaret LaFrance, 2003

Selected Poetry of William Blake: edited by Ian Lancashire; from the University of Toronto Libraries

William Blake: the companion site to the Tate Britain's 2000-01 Blake exhibition, containing links to digital representations of the Tate's Blake collection and to its educational project, Blake Interactive

William Blake's Final Resting Place: by Luis and Carol Garrido; from the Friends of William Blake

Romanticism and Related Literary Studies

British Association for Romantic Studies (BARS): promotes the study of the cultural history of the Romantic period through conferences, research assistance, and an electronic mailbase

Bible Gateway: a searchable database containing multiple versions of the Bible

18thConnect: Eighteenth-Century Scholarship Online: provides users with access to peer-reviewed scholarly content and the ability to search digital archives (including ECCO) containing primary source material; a sister-organization of NINES

Internet Library of Early Journals: a digital collection of selected 18th- and 19th-century journals; compiled by the Universities of Birmingham, Leeds, Manchester, and Oxford

NINES: Nineteenth-Century Scholarship Online: "a scholarly organization devoted to forging links between the material archive of the nineteenth century and the digital research environment of the twenty-first;" a sister-organization of 18thConnect

North American Society for the Study of Romanticism (NASSR): provides a forum for the discussion of a wide variety of theoretical approaches to Romantic works of all genres, nations, and disciplines

Romantic Circles: an extensive research site devoted to the study of Romantic-period literature and culture; edited by Neil Fraistat and Steven E. Jones; from the University of Maryland

Romantic London: allows users to explore the life and culture of Romantic London through the use of interactive maps; by Matthew Sangster

Romanticism and Victorianism On the Net (RaVoN): an international quarterly academic journal; edited by Michael E. Sinatra and Jason Camlot

The Rossetti Archive: edited by Jerome J. McGann

The Shelley-Godwin Archive: from the New York Public Library and the Maryland Institute for Technology in the Humanities

Art and Art History

The Brilliant Line: an interactive site allowing users to explore the process of engraving; from the Rhode Island School of Design

Graphics Atlas: an online resource providing an "object-based approach for the identification and characterization of prints and photographs;" from the Rochester Institute of Technology

The John Linnell Archive: from the Fitzwilliam Museum

Oxford Art Online: "a virtual art reference library" [subscription required]

Sistine Chapel: contains historical information, images, and a virtual tour; part of Vatican Museums Online
